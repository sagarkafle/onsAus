Duct/Exhaust Fan Cleaning

The duct that leads from the hood to the exhaust fan is the crucial part of exhaust system that is often neglected. It is very imperative for the ducting to be cleaned to the required Australian Standard.

We provide thorough duct cleaning service and even install an access panel in order to facilitate an access to inside if there is not a panel already installed. The duct that has not been cleaned over an extended period of time possess serious fire hazard and put you in a risk of not being compliant in case of any fire incidents. We thoroughly clean the entire duct leading from the canopy to the exhaust fan and the fan itself.

How you benefit from the Duct/Exhaust Fan cleaning?
  • Comply with Business Insurance.
  • Comply with OH&S obligations.
  • Comply with required Australian Standard.
  • Better air flow, clean environment within the kitchen.
  • Reduced fire risks.
  • Improved ventilation for smoke and odour control.
