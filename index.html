Why choose ONS?

We know you have a lot of choices when it comes to choosing who to clean
your home, here are some reasons to consider CleanPro.

Safety

Safety is another part of operation we take very seriously. We are committed to safety of everyone whilst on site and only perform the job adhering to WH&S act. A site-specific SWMS is prepared for any high-risk work to be carried out.

Quality

Quality is one of the most essential things that we take care of and we guarantee you won’t find any other company that can do any job better than us. The Kitchen Exhaust systems are cleaned to the AS 1851.6-2012 and we provide you with detailed report of job completion including the Compliance Certificate.

Professional and Specialized Cleaning

ONS Australia is one of the fastest growing cleaning company specialising in Kitchen and Kitchen exhausts. With years of experience and team of qualified technicians, you can trust us for any job regardless of small or big. We are committed to quality and safety to the highest degree.

Cutting Edge Technology and Equipment

We use latest technologies and equipment to ensure that you get the best value and top notch service and with no any third parties involved in between the process, you can trust us for your next service.
